About PolicySpot

PolicySpot is the official policy documentation portal for Global Furry Television (GFTV). This site hosts the Charter of Global Furry Television and related policy documents in a structured, accessible format.

About GFTV

Global Furry Television (国际兽圈电视) is a Singapore-based furry fandom media channel dedicated to producing and distributing furry fandom entertainment and news content, and facilitating cultural and community activity and exchange among participants of the furry fandom.

Document

The Charter of Global Furry Television (Edition 2026) is the supreme governing framework of GFTV, establishing its ethical and operational principles across nine articles and one schedule.
